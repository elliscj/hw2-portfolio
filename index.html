About Me

My name is Cameron Ellis. I am an aspiring Software Developer based out of Denver CO. I have lived in the wonderful state of Colorado for my whole life and do not currently have any plans to leave, but will for the right price...wink wink. For the past 6 years I have worked as a Laser Technician for a company that makes laser engraving and cutting machines based out of Golden CO. This job was fun and rewarding for a time, but was not the career path I had hoped for, which has led me to looking for a new career in the field of Software Delvelopment. I am an extremely hard worker who believes that you get out what you put in and have an acute attention to detail. My favorite hobbies are motorcycle travel and golf. Traveling and being 'one' with the environment and your machine is something that not many other methods of travel give you. My dream trip is to ride from Alaska to Ushuaia on the southern tip of Argentina. And my dog Amos III, I love my dog.
